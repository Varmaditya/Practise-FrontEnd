Dr. Carl Sagan

The man who revolutionised Astronomy as an entertainment.

Dr. Carl Edward Sagan was an American astronomer, planetary scientist, and science communicator.

Here's a time line of Dr. Sagan's life:

  • 1934 - Born in Brooklyn, New York
  • 1350 - Sagan started working as an advisor for NASA
  • 1955 - Carl sagan gets his bachelor's degree at the University of Chicago
  • 1957 - Carl Sagan married his first wife Lynn Margulis
  • 1960 - Carl Sagan taught at the University of Harvard
  • 1962 - Mariner 2 confired Sagan's views that venus was dry and very hot
  • 1967 - Sagan and Pollack solved mystery about the seasonal "wave or darkening" on mars
  • 1968 - Sagan married his second wife Linda Salzman
  • 1971 - Became a full professor at Cornell Univsersity
  • 1972 - Became associate director of the center for the radio physics and space research at cornell
  • 1975 - Sagan decided to test the capacity of television to bring science to a mass audience
  • 1976 - Sagan became a founding member of the committee for the scientific investigation of claims of the paranormal
  • 1980 - The Documentary called "Cosmos: A Spacetime Odyssey" was released to the public on the TV with its host Carl Sagan
  • 1981 - Carl Sagan married his third wife Ann Druyan
  • 1996 - Sagan died of a rare bone marrow disease
